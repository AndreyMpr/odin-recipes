Plug and Play Takoyaki

Description

Burns your tongue, your mouth, and a huge sphere-shaped hole in your heart.
These takoyaki are as good as you can make them. That's why they aren't that great on the photo.
But you can do it better!

Ingredients

  • Tako......RIP
  • Eggs......multiple
  • Takoyaki sauce......for special effects

Steps

  1. Take a bunch of eggs. I don't know how many, to be honest. Just enough so that you can put it in a deep bowl and stir it without making too much of a clanking noise.
  2. I think maybe some flour involved, but just a little.
  3. Cut tako in very small bits. That majestic, very smart and seemingly self-aware animal.
  4. Go through a series of existential moral dilemas.
  5. Pour that egg-flower mix into a takoyaki-maker.
  6. Stick a piece of tako inside every takoyaki.
  7. As one side gets fried, turn it over with a toothpick, to get the full sphere shape on every takoyaki.
  8. Prepare cold beverages for the moment when you inevitably burn your mouth.
  9. Put some takoyaki sauce and those fish flakes that make it look like it's all moving about.
  10. お召し上がりください。
