Suspiria omuraisu

Discription

This omuraisu is made with love. It tastes good and it's not normal.
If you are bored of eating nice looking food, try this body-horror omuraisu.

Ingredients

  • Rice......some amount
  • Egg......several
  • Chicken......hopefully
  • Ketchup......use your imagination
  • Moe moe......kyun kyun
  • Blue plate......and one fork

Steps

  1. Take already cooked rice and put it onto a frying pan.
  2. Begin to cook it
  3. Add chicken that you had left from the previous dinner
  4. Fry some eggs separately and then merge them with rice
  5. Add cryptic message to make it creepier
