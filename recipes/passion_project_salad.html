Passion Project Salad

Discription

This is a perfect food when you are busy with school during the day, and move boxes whole night at a logistical center.
But when you finally get a minute for yourself, you want to get fancy. It's easy to make, pick with a fork and digest.
Trully unforgetable gastronomical experience that no one should ever try to repeat.
So here's a recipe for The Passion Project Salad.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers......1
  • Bell Pepper......1
  • Salad......whatever you have
  • Chicken......hopefully
  • Misery sauce......probably for takoyaki
  • Inspiration......to eat something other than cup ramen

Steps

  1. Take cucumber and bell pepper, cut them so you can easily pick two-three pieces with the fork.
  2. Sprinkle some salad that you have left from the day you decided to be a vegan, but then gave up and ate a sausage.
  3. Cut chicken filet into similar cmall chunks
  4. Put it all in a plate and squeeze some sause on it
  5. Bon appetite
